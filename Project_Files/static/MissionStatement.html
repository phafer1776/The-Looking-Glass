Our Mission

The Looking Glass is a user-driven, open-source community that allows people from all over the world to share their unique photographs with one another. From professional photographers to amateurs to people who just believe they’ve taken a good picture, The Looking Glass is open to anyone who wants to share their work with the world!

Our mission is simple: Connect people who connect with art. A picture is worth a thousand words, so we like to think that our website is more of a dictionary. So Join, Contribute, and Download! We are excited for you to experience a new world through our Looking Glass.
